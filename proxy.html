How to use the Proxy

To use the proxy, follow these steps:

Drag the button below into to your browser's bookmarks bar:

Proxy

Now, when you visit a webpage you want to use the proxy on, simply click the bookmark button that says "Proxy" in your bookmarks bar.
Keep in mind that you can change the name of the bookmark at any time by right-clicking the bookmark, clicking "Edit", and updating the name in the box, then clicking "Save."

What to do if you don't see the Bookmark bar

If you don't see the bookmark bar at the top of your screen, then you can activate it by just pressing Ctrl, Shift, and the letter B all at the same time!
